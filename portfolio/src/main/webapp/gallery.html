Chicago's LightScape

The Lightscape at Chicago is a yearly event that is hosted during the months of December and January. It takes place in the Chicago Botanic Gardens. The whole Lightscape takes about an hour to go through, but considering how the trail is only one mile long, you can imagine that most people take their time to take in all the beautiful views.
